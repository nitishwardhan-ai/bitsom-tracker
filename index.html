FAQs

Can I bring my family members with me?
Family members are not allowed to participate in or attend the immersion sessions. They may accompany you for travel purposes up to the campus location.
Is there any fee for attending?
No fee for the immersion itself — breakfast, lunch and snacks are provided by BITSoM. Students taking on-campus accommodation pay ₹4,000, which includes food and stay.
How is accommodation confirmed?
Select "Yes" in the form, pay ₹4,000 via the payment link and upload your screenshot. Our team verifies payments daily — you'll receive a separate accommodation confirmation email once verified. Only 20 accommodation slots exist; check the counter above before paying.
What if accommodation is full?
You can still attend the full immersion without a campus stay — meals on both days remain free. Any excess accommodation payments will be refunded.
Will I get a certificate? What's the agenda?
Yes — all participants receive a campus immersion participation certificate. The detailed agenda will be communicated one week prior. Academic sessions focus on Building AI Agents.
Is there a dress code?
No strict dress code. Smart casuals and comfortable footwear are encouraged; carry clothing appropriate for local weather.
Is parking available on campus?
Limited parking may be available, first come, first served. 4-wheeler parking will not be available.
What about my regular online classes?
No regular online classes are scheduled during the immersion dates.
If I don't attend, will I get recordings?
No — sessions are in-person only and will not be recorded.
Do I need to bring my laptop?
Yes, a laptop is mandatory.
